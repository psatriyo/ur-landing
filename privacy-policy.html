Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Learn how we protect your data with our privacy-first approach.

Last Updated: November 29, 2025
💾

Local-First Storage

All your financial data is stored locally on your device by default. No external servers, no cloud databases we control.

🚫

Zero Data Collection

We don't collect analytics, usage data, or any personal information. No tracking pixels, no third-party SDKs.

☁️

Optional iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, data goes to your personal iCloud account - controlled by Apple and your Apple ID, not us.

🚫

No Ads, No Selling

We will never show ads or sell your data. Your financial information is never monetized.

🔒 Our Privacy Commitment

UangRumah is built with privacy at its core. We believe your financial data belongs to you - and only you. Unlike most apps, we don't collect, track, or sell your personal information.

💾 Data Stored on Your Device

UangRumah stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Expense records (name, amount, vendor, date, category, cost center)
  • Budget settings and alert thresholds
  • Custom categories and cost centers you create
  • Vendor history for suggestions
  • App preferences and settings
  • Recurring expense configurations

☁️ iCloud Synchronization

When you enable iCloud sync, your expense data is backed up to your personal iCloud account. This allows you to access your data across multiple Apple devices. Important points:

  • iCloud sync is disabled by default - you must explicitly enable it
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
  • We have no access to your iCloud data
  • Your data is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy
  • You can disable sync or delete iCloud data anytime through iOS Settings

🚫 What We Never Do

Zero Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal or financial data with any third parties.

  • Collect personal identification information
  • Track your location or behavior
  • Use analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • Include advertising SDKs or networks
  • Sell or share data with third parties
  • Send data to our servers (we don't have any)

🔐 Your Data Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app anytime
  • Export: Download your data in PDF or CSV format
  • Modify: Edit or update any record at any time
  • Delete: Remove individual items or all data
  • Portability: Take your data with you through exports

🔒 Security Measures

We take multiple steps to keep your data secure:

  • iOS secure containers protect local storage
  • iCloud encryption for synced data
  • Privacy Mode to hide sensitive information in public
  • No vulnerable backend servers to hack
  • Regular updates to address security concerns

Questions About Privacy?

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at support@uangrumah.com. We respond within 7 business days.
