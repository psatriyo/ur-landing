Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your financial data.

Last Updated: November 17, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to UangRumah. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your financial data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our iOS expense tracking application.

UangRumah is designed specifically for Indonesian couples to manage household expenses efficiently. We believe in transparency and want you to understand exactly how your data is handled.

2. Information We Collect

UangRumah collects and stores the following types of information locally on your device and optionally in your iCloud account:

Financial Data

  • Expense Records: Transaction details including expense name, vendor, amount, date, category, and cost center
  • Budget Information: Your monthly and weekly budget limits and alert threshold preferences
  • Categories: Custom expense categories you create, including names, icons, and color preferences
  • Cost Centers: Payment method information including names and types (bank accounts, credit cards, cash, e-wallets)
  • Recurring Expenses: Automated expense tracking data for subscriptions and regular bills
  • Vendors: Names and details of merchants where you make purchases

Application Settings

  • Preferences: Your app settings including privacy mode preferences, notification settings, and reminder schedules
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app to improve functionality (stored locally only)

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: All data is stored locally on your device by default. No data is sent to external servers or third-party services unless you explicitly enable iCloud sync.

3. iCloud Synchronization

UangRumah offers optional iCloud synchronization to keep your data in sync across your Apple devices (iPhone and iPad).

How iCloud Sync Works

  • When enabled, your expense data, budgets, categories, and cost centers are automatically backed up to your personal iCloud account
  • Data is encrypted during transmission and storage using Apple's security standards
  • Only you can access this data through devices signed in to your iCloud account
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data - it is controlled entirely by Apple and your iCloud security settings
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time through iOS Settings

iCloud Privacy

When using iCloud sync, your data is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy and iCloud terms. We recommend reviewing Apple's privacy practices at apple.com/legal/privacy.

4. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively within the UangRumah app for the following purposes:

  • Expense Tracking: To record, display, and organize your financial transactions
  • Budget Management: To calculate budget progress, daily allowances, and trigger spending alerts
  • Analytics & Insights: To generate dashboard visualizations, category breakdowns, and spending reports
  • Reminders: To send you notifications based on your configured preferences (expense nudges, budget alerts, monthly summaries)
  • Data Export: To create PDF and CSV reports when you request them
  • App Improvement: To understand feature usage and improve app functionality (locally only, no data transmission)

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

üîí Zero Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal or financial data with any third parties. Period.

UangRumah operates with complete data privacy:

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No social media integration
  • No data brokers or marketing companies
  • No cloud servers except your personal iCloud (if you enable it)

The only data transmission occurs when:

  • You enable iCloud sync (data goes only to your iCloud account)
  • You export reports (data stays on your device or goes where you choose to share it)

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your financial data:

Technical Safeguards

  • Local Storage: Data is stored in iOS secure containers protected by device encryption
  • iCloud Encryption: When sync is enabled, data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Privacy Mode: Built-in features to blur sensitive information and censor amounts when needed
  • No Backend Servers: No vulnerable web servers or databases that could be hacked
  • iOS Security: Leverages Apple's robust security infrastructure and regular security updates

Device Security Recommendations

  • Use a strong passcode or biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID) on your device
  • Keep your iOS software up to date
  • Enable two-factor authentication for your Apple ID/iCloud account
  • Don't jailbreak your device, as it compromises security

7. Your Data Control Rights

You have complete control over your data in UangRumah:

Access and Portability

  • View All Data: Access all your expense records, budgets, and settings within the app
  • Export Data: Export your financial data in PDF or CSV format at any time
  • Search and Filter: Use advanced search features to find specific transactions

Modification and Deletion

  • Edit Records: Modify any expense, budget, category, or cost center at any time
  • Delete Data: Remove individual expenses or bulk delete records
  • Version History: View and restore previous versions of edited expenses
  • Complete Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all local data from your device

iCloud Data Management

  • Disable iCloud sync in iOS Settings to stop cloud synchronization
  • Delete iCloud data through iOS Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Manage Storage > UangRumah
  • Data deletion from iCloud may take up to 30 days to complete across Apple's servers

8. Children's Privacy

UangRumah is designed for adults managing household expenses. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of any material changes through:

  • In-app notifications when you update to a new version
  • Updates to this page with a revised "Last Updated" date
  • Email notification (if you have provided contact information for support)

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

10. International Users

UangRumah is designed primarily for users in Indonesia and displays amounts in Indonesian Rupiah (IDR). However, the app can be used globally. Please note:

  • All data processing occurs locally on your device or in your iCloud account
  • iCloud data storage location is determined by your Apple ID country/region settings
  • We comply with applicable data protection regulations including GDPR where applicable
